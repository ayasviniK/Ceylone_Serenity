Frequently Asked Questions

We offer free cancellation up to 30 days before travel for a full refund. Cancellations 15–29 days prior receive a 50% refund. Within 14 days, a cancellation fee applies. We recommend travel insurance for added peace of mind.
Most nationalities require an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) prior to arrival. The ETA can be obtained online within minutes at eta.gov.lk. We recommend applying at least 48 hours before departure.
Sri Lanka is a year-round destination! For the South & West coast beaches and Cultural Triangle, December–April is peak season. For the East coast beaches, May–September is ideal. Wildlife safaris are best from January–April.
