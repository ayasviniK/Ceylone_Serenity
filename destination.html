Trending places

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Experience the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya, one of Sri Lanka's most iconic landmarks.

Yala National Park

Explore the wildlife of Yala National Park, home to leopards, elephants, and a diverse array of bird species.

Mirissa

Relax on the pristine sands of Mirissa Beach, known for its breathtaking sunsets and crystal-clear waters.

Ella

Enjoy the scenic beauty of Ella, known for its lush hills, tea plantations, and the famous Nine Arches Bridge.

Temple of the Tooth

Visit the sacred sanctuary in the heart of Kandy, a crucial piece of Sri Lankan heritage.

Dambulla Cave Temple

Explore the stunning ancient rock temples filled with historic Buddhist statues and murals.

Nine Arches Bridge

Witness the architectural marvel of the colonial-era viaduct hidden deep in the jungle.

Arugam Bay

Catch the perfect wave at one of the world's renowned surfing destinations on the east coast.
